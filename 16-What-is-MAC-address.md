# What is MAC address?

MAC stands for **Media Access Control** address, it is also known as physical address or hardware address.
MAC address is a unique and permanent address for all electronic and networking devices.

## MAC address format -

1. MM:MM:MM:SS:SS:SS
2. MM-MM-MM-SS-SS-SS
3. MM.MM.MM.SS.SS.SS

**Note** - **M** represents Manifacturer and **S** represents the Service

**Example** - 3C:D9:2B:6f:2b:9c

*A MAC address is a 12 Digit Hexadecimal number and toal length of MAC address is 48 bits*

- First 6 digist are for manifacturer and other 6 are provided for NIC

## USE -

1. Device tracking
2. MAC address needed for connecting any device because it is an identification of system
3. We can secure our network through MAC address (We can block any other connected device)
