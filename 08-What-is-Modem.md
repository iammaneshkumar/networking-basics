# What is modem?

Model stands for modulator & demodulator, it is a network device that is placed between the computer system and telephone line.
It has two part, Modulator and Demodulator. **Modulator** convert digital signal to analog signal and **Demodulator** converts analog signal to digital signal.

**Note -** It allow us to computer to connect internet

## Advantages of Modem

1. Provides signal to signal data sending and receiving (like Digital to Digital)
2. Long distance data sharing.
3. Transmission speed in high.

## Disadvantages of Modem

1. Wire is required for sharing data.
2. Speed wil be slow for long distance data sharing.
